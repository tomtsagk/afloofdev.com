## Dargite3D

`linux` | `keyboard` | `C++`, `OpenGL`

<img id="icon" src="/images/icon_dargite.png" alt="Dargite3d icon"/>

Pong clone. Pick from 12 available characters, 
and some available stages. Moddable enough to allow adding new characters,
and new stages.
